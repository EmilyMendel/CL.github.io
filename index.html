Pantai Mangrove

Function: Mangrove forests are one of the places that can guard the border between land and sea areas.
Mangrove forests have the following functions: Preventing Beach Erosion. Beach erosion or abrasion is a threat to the environment. Become a Soil Catalyst from Seawater. Environment Small animals in the sea. Providing Broad Economic Impact. Animal Feed Sources. Maintain Water and Air Quality.
Products: Mangrove fruit can be processed into flour and various kinds processed food ingredients such as syrup, chips, dodol, and other processed snacks.
